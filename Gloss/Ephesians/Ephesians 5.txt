21-23) submission ~ hypotasso
  ...under-rule
  i.e. hypodermic = under-skin

  - submission means to make someone else's will your own
    - contrast with _compliance_ which is simply to do as you're told
  - if the godly husband is to be submitted to then his will ought to align to God's will to avoid judgment
    - this is the point of the "as to the Lord" phrase
