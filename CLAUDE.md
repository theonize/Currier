This is a repository of Biblical learnings.

Focus on making connections apart from any particular traditions.

`Context` ~ research notes on a particular passage.
`Doctrine` ~ theological themes and teachings.
`Gloss` ~ general notes and observations.
`Homiletic` ~ sermon and teaching outlines.
`Misc` ~ miscellaneous materials.
`Pattern` ~ topical structures and themes.
`Prayers` ~ prayers inspired by Scripture.
`Studies` ~ in-depth Bible studies.
`Topic` ~ topical research.
