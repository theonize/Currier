# Patterns of Prayer

## AASP

The four steps:
Anchor — Start by focusing on and declaring the receiver's (God's) nature, power, and character. This reportedly shifts the pray-er from stress/fear to a receptive state (high correlation with positive outcomes).
Alignment — Ask for something that serves a bigger purpose beyond pure self-interest (e.g., wisdom to lead others, not just personal gain).
Surrender Paradox — Make the request while fully releasing attachment to the specific outcome ("not my will, but yours").
Persistence — Keep at it over time, even through delays (miracles averaged ~23 days in the analysis).
